# Kevin MacLeod

> **Note**: Referenced by [[Scott Moehring]] as inspiration for [[Remixability]] concept during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

## Who is Kevin MacLeod?

**"The most prolific composer you've never heard of"** - New York Times

## Achievements

- Created **2,000 pieces** of royalty-free music
- Released under **Creative Commons** license
- One song: **31.6 billion plays**
- Used in countless TikTok videos, Facebook Reels, YouTube videos

## The Model

- Freely distributes music for anyone to use
- No licensing fees
- Attribution requested but flexible
- Music becomes "ingredients" for others' creations

## Impact on Remixability

Inspired Scott's concept of creating **ingredients rather than finished products**:
- Enable others to create
- Give work "a life of its own"
- Value use over control
- Massive reach through openness
