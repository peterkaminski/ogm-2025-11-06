## Net Positive Concept

Going beyond:
- **Harmful**: Causing damage
- **Less harmful**: Reducing damage
- **Neutral**: No net impact
- **Sustainable**: Maintaining current state

To:
- **Net Positive**: Creating more value than consumed
- Active regeneration
- Leaving things better
- Contributing to flourishing
