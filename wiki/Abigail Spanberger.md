# Abigail Spanberger

> **Note**: Mentioned by [[Mike Nelson]] as newly elected Virginia governor during discussion of [[Political Shifts 2025]] on the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

## Background

- Former U.S. Congressperson
- Known personally by [[Mike Nelson]] for ~6 years
- Close with one of Mike's best friends

## Character

Mike described her as:
- "Moral, decent, smart, kind person"
- "A young person" (representing generational shift)

## Election Victory (November 2025)

Won Virginia governorship as part of Democratic landslide that also captured:
- Lieutenant Governor position
- Attorney General position
- 14-seat legislative majority (up from 1-seat)

## Significance

- Positioned to resist Trump administration overreach
- Attorney General role crucial for filing lawsuits
- One 4-year term only (Virginia's unique system)
- Example of talent development system working
