Panoptica.ai

Note: See Panoptica and Narrative Tracking for full treatment of this tool mentioned by Gil Friend during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

Quick Summary

New site by Ben Hunt and Rusty Gwynn that:

  • Scans ~8,000 websites globally with AI
  • Tracks narrative trends
  • Shows rise and fall of conversations
  • Provides "meta-view on stories that power the world"
