# Schumacher Institute

> **Note**: UK organization mentioned by [[Klaus Mager]] as partner for his [[Regenerative Agriculture]] project during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

## What is Schumacher Institute?

UK-based organization (likely inspired by E.F. Schumacher, author of "Small is Beautiful")

## Klaus's Project

Hosting initiative with two work streams:

### 1. Food Hubs into Wholesale Markets
- Helping small-to-medium farmers access markets
- Bundling/aggregating farmers
- Getting "agriculture of the middle" into wholesale channels

### 2. Paying for Ecosystem Services
- Creating secondary revenue streams for farmers
- Paying for environmental benefits
- Soil health, cover crops, etc.
- Making regenerative practices economically viable

## Significance

Partner organization bringing credibility and infrastructure to scaling regenerative agriculture in UK.
