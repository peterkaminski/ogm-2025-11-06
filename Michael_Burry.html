Michael Burry

Note: Referenced during AI Financial Bubble discussion on the OGM 2025-11-06 call as someone currently shorting tech stocks.

Who is Michael Burry?

"Real-Life Market Genius From The Big Short" - subject of Michael Lewis's book and subsequent film.

Famous For

Successfully predicted and profited from the 2008 housing market collapse by shorting mortgage-backed securities.

Current Activity (2025)

Has a fund set up to short the tech sector, specifically:

  • NVIDIA
  • Palantir
  • Other AI-related stocks

The Palantir CEO Incident

When interviewed about Burry shorting Palantir:

  • CEO Alex Karp looked "panicked"
  • "Oh, this is really bad. Oh, this is not fair, oh..."
  • "Deer in the headlights" - Doug Breitbart
  • Stock price dropped 12%

The Timing Problem

New Yorker article from 2016: "Michael Burry... Thinks Another Financial Crisis Is Looming"

If you acted on that warning, you would have:

  • Missed a decade of market growth
  • Eventually run out of money from shorting

Jerry's point: "Timing is everything in these things. And it's a little nutty."

The Question

Can someone be a short for a long time and still be successful? History suggests it's very difficult.
