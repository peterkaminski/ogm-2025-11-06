Noetic Science

Note: Referenced by Jerry Michalski during his discussion of Non-Local Consciousness on the OGM 2025-11-06 call as "woo-woo noetic science territory, which I'm beginning to respect a lot more."

What is Noetic Science?

Noetic Sciences is an interdisciplinary field exploring consciousness, human potential, and the relationship between mind and matter. The term comes from the Greek "noesis" (inner knowing or intuitive consciousness).

Key Areas

  • Consciousness studies
  • Meditation and contemplative practices
  • Exceptional human experiences
  • Mind-body connection
  • Parapsychology and psi phenomena
  • Integral approaches to knowing

Institute of Noetic Sciences (IONS)

Founded in 1973 by Apollo 14 astronaut Edgar Mitchell after his profound experience viewing Earth from space, recognizing consciousness as fundamental.

Connection to the Call

Jerry's evolving view of the field in relation to:

  • Non-Local Consciousness theories
  • Dan Brown's treatment in "The Secret of Secrets"
  • His own Psilocybin and Perception experience
  • Mainstream vs "woo-woo" perspectives

The "Woo-Woo" Label

Jerry acknowledged the field's reputation as "woo-woo" (dismissive term for beliefs considered mystical or unscientific) but noted he's "beginning to respect a lot more."

This reflects:

  • Growing scientific interest in consciousness
  • Personal experiences validating non-materialist views
  • Integration of mystical and scientific perspectives
