Dan Brown

Note: Author discussed by Jerry Michalski during his check-in about Non-Local Consciousness on the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

Who is Dan Brown?

Bestselling author known for thrillers that weave historical mysteries, symbols, and controversial ideas into page-turning fiction.

Books Discussed in Call

The Secret of Secrets (Latest)

  • Major plot point: Non-Local Consciousness
  • Discusses GABA and filtering
  • Explores psilocybin and perception
  • Jerry: "Brown is not Faulkner" but valuable for introducing ideas

The Da Vinci Code

  • Thesis: Marginalization of the Divine Feminine
  • Parallel to Leonard Shlain's academic work
  • Brought controversial ideas to mainstream

Origin (2017)

  • Robert Langdon series, Book 5
  • Features rogue AI
  • Mentioned by David Witzel

Jerry's Appreciation

While acknowledging Brown isn't literary fiction, Jerry values how Brown:

  • Brings complex ideas to popular audience
  • Makes "woo-woo" concepts accessible
  • Weaves real research into entertaining stories
  • Introduces readers to fascinating theories
