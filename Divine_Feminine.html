Divine Feminine

Note: Concept discussed by Jerry Michalski as connecting multiple threads in his thinking during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

The Concept

Marginalization of the Divine Feminine - thesis that:

  • Goddess worship was systematically suppressed
  • Feminine principles devalued in Western culture
  • Patriarchy displaced balanced perspectives
  • Historical revisionism

Sources Jerry Connected

  1. Dan Brown's Da Vinci Code - Popular fiction treatment
  2. Leonard Shlain's "The Alphabet vs. the Goddess" - Academic analysis
  3. Jerry's own work - Decades studying "consumer" concept
  4. Yin and Yang - Balance of masculine/feminine principles

The Click

"All three stories kind of clicked in my head in a really wonderful way"

Same thesis from completely different perspectives validated the pattern.

Connection to Other Ideas

Part of Jerry's larger synthesis that later included:

  • Non-Local Consciousness
  • GABA and filtering
  • Broader questions about perception and reality
