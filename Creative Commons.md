# Creative Commons

> **Note**: Licensing framework mentioned in context of [[Kevin MacLeod]]'s music during [[Scott Moehring]]'s [[Remixability]] discussion.

## What is Creative Commons?

Set of copyright licenses that allow creators to grant specific permissions for others to use their work.

## Kevin MacLeod's Use

Released 2,000 pieces of music under Creative Commons, enabling:
- Free use by others
- Massive distribution (31.6 billion plays for one song)
- Remixing and adaptation
- Building on his work

## Connection to Remixability

Perfect example of:
- Creating "ingredients" not just finished products
- Enabling others' creativity
- Value through use, not control
- [[Remixability]] in practice
