Critical Thinking Frameworks

Note: Implicit category from Stacey Druss's Two Truths and a Lie approach discussed during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

Stacey's Framework

Two Truths and a Lie: Assume every story contains both truth and falsehood, always working to discern which is which.

Why Frameworks Matter

In an age of:

  • Information Overload
  • Conflicting narratives
  • AI Bias and Perspectives
  • Seeking Certainty

Need tools to:

  • Navigate complexity
  • Maintain open mind
  • Avoid both cynicism and gullibility
  • Make progress despite uncertainty

Other Implied Frameworks from the Call

Nelson's Law: Nothing improves unless it changes (but not every change is improvement)

Environmental Neurodiversity: Reframing overwhelm as environmental mismatch

Remixability: Optimizing for use over control

Field of Sheaves: Synthesizing across frameworks
