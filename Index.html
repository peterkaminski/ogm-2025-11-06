OGM 2025-11-06 Community Call

Welcome to the wiki documentation for the OGM (Open Global Mind) community call held on Thursday, November 6, 2025.

Overview

This was a first-Thursday check-in call where participants shared what "OGM-y" things they've been working on or thinking about. The call featured rich discussions on AI, consciousness, regenerative agriculture, financial bubbles, and the nature of curiosity.

Participants

  • Scott Moehring - Remixability and making work as ingredients
  • Gil Friend - Automated transcript de-identification and sustainability
  • Alex Kladitis - AI bias in news reporting
  • Pete Kaminski - Agentic AI for document synthesis
  • Kevin Jones - The Plex publication
  • David Witzel - Growth in living systems vs economics
  • Klaus Mager - Incentivizing regenerative agriculture
  • Jerry Michalski - Non-local consciousness, GABA, and psilocybin
  • Mike Nelson - Overstimulation and political shifts
  • Victoria (Spain) - The drop in curiosity
  • Stacey Druss - Two truths and a lie approach to certainty
  • Doug Breitbart - Tab groups as tab emancipation
  • Rick Botelho - Listening (did not check in)
