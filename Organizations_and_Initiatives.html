Agricultural & Environmental

Schumacher Institute

  • Location: United Kingdom
  • Project: Hosting Klaus Mager's initiative with two work streams:
    1. Getting food hubs into wholesale markets
    2. Paying farmers for ecosystem services
  • Context: Regenerative Agriculture economic models
  • Mentioned by: Klaus Mager
  • See: Klaus Mager, Regenerative Agriculture

Food with Thought

  • Who: Klaus Mager's effort
  • Focus: Engaging "the neglected part of the market" - small to medium-sized farmers
  • Goal: Help "agriculture of the middle" access markets
  • Secondary Goal: Create revenue stream for environmental services
  • Mentioned by: Klaus Mager
  • See: Klaus Mager

Union of Concerned Scientists

  • Activity: Sending "last urgent warnings" about climate
  • Frequency: For 3-4 years
  • Hub: Portland State or Oregon State (consolidating data)
  • Message: "Passing one tipping point after the next"
  • Klaus's Frustration: Warnings ignored, need transition to action
  • Mentioned by: Klaus Mager
  • See: Klaus Mager

CSA (Community Supported Agriculture)

  • Definition: Partnership between farmers and consumers
  • Model: People buy "share" of farm's harvest in advance
  • Return: Regular boxes/baskets of fresh, seasonal produce (+ sometimes eggs, dairy, meat, flowers)
  • Frequency: Usually weekly or biweekly throughout growing season
  • Market Share: Combined with farmers markets = only ~3% of total food sales
  • Defined by: Pete Kaminski in chat
  • Context: Klaus Mager's discussion of market access
  • See: Klaus Mager
