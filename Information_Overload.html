Information Overload

Note: Underlying theme throughout discussions of Curiosity and Attention, Environmental Neurodiversity, and Overstimulation and Environmental Neurodiversity during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

The Phenomenon

Modern condition where:

  • Constant information streams
  • Multiple simultaneous channels
  • No empty cognitive space
  • Always-on connectivity
  • Rapid context switching

Scott's Observation

"We have killed boredom with the constant stream of information everywhere"

Result: Loss of available attention, appearing as curiosity decline

Mike's Experience

Feeling "completely, absolutely, intellectually, politically overstimulated" from:

  • TEDx Mid-Atlantic
  • Virginia elections
  • Technology developments
  • AI bubble concerns
  • General information flood

Impacts Discussed

On Curiosity

  • Less available attention
  • Engagement declining despite curiosity
  • Appearing as lack of interest

On Filtering

  • Typical filters inadequate
  • GABA and Filtering working overtime
  • Need for new coping strategies

On Neurodiversity

  • Neurotypical people experiencing what neurodiverse always have
  • Environmental Neurodiversity hypothesis
