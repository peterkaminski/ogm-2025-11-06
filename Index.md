# OGM 2025-11-06 Community Call

Welcome to the wiki documentation for the OGM (Open Global Mind) community call held on Thursday, November 6, 2025.

## Overview

This was a first-Thursday check-in call where participants shared what "OGM-y" things they've been working on or thinking about. The call featured rich discussions on AI, consciousness, regenerative agriculture, financial bubbles, and the nature of curiosity.
