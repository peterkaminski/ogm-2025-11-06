# Michael Lewis

> **Note**: Author mentioned by [[Jerry Michalski]], [[Gil Friend]], and [[Mike Nelson]] during discussions of The Big Short and [[Michael Burry]].

## Who is Michael Lewis?

**"Brilliant"** author (Mike Nelson's description) known for narrative non-fiction that makes complex topics accessible.

## Books Mentioned

### The Big Short
- About the 2008 housing market collapse
- Features [[Michael Burry]]
- Jerry: "Among my favorite bits of media like that. They're just so insightful"
- Made into successful film

### Moneyball
- Oakland A's and baseball analytics
- Also a major film

### Other Works
Gil: "Done a series of books of similar depth and insight, really terrific"
