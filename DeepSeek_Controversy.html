DeepSeek Controversy

Note: Chinese AI discussed by Alex Kladitis as parallel to Western AI bias during the OGM 2025-11-06 call.

What is DeepSeek?

Chinese AI system that emerged as competitor to Western LLMs

Western Criticism

Media criticized it for:

  • Being "biased towards pro-China stuff"
  • Censoring Tiananmen Square
  • Not "behaving by our standards"

Alex's Point

Used this as comparison to show Western AIs also have bias:

  • DeepSeek's bias was obvious to Western observers
  • ChatGPT's bias less obvious to Western users
  • Both systems reflect their training contexts

The Insight

"Our AIs are also quite opinionated in certain directions"
